About

I’m a professor in the Department of Business & Information Systems at Bronx Community College (CUNY). My work focuses on student success, curriculum design, program assessment, and career-aligned pathways.

I collaborate with faculty, staff, and external partners to strengthen experiential learning and support continuous improvement across business and information systems programs.
